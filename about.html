About Me

I’m Manyu, a UX/UI designer from Philly.

Design has always been something I’m genuinely passionate about. To me, design is more than visual presentation; it’s a way to communicate and solve problems. I enjoy creating intuitive and meaningful experiences, and I hope the work I create can support users, especially when they’re facing confusing or frustrating moments.

As a UX/UI designer, I approach every project with curiosity and a willingness to experiment. I enjoy learning how different tools, methods, and interactions shape the final experience, and I treat each project as an opportunity to push myself a little further. I believe that good design comes from exploring many possibilities, choosing the best solution, and refining it until it truly works for the people who need it.

When I’m not designing, you may find me staying connected to creativity in my own ways. I often spend time drawing or making handicrafts, which lets me turn my thoughts into something tangible. I love exploring artworks from different artists to improve my sense of aesthetics, and I also learn from great designers and their thinking processes.
